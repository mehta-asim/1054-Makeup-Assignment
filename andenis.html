Home

I am from Jamaica. It is a small English speaking island in the Caribbean. While we speak English, Patois is the language that is commonly spoken. Patois is a mixture of English, Spanish, French and Afriacn words spoken together. Jamaica is home to reggae icon, Bob Marley and the fastest man in the world, Usain Bolt.
